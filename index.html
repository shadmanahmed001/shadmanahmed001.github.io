About

Shadman Ahmed serves as Resident Scholar and Imam at Masjid Zakariya (ICF) in Fremont, California, with a focus on accessible Islamic learning, family-centered guidance, and principled community leadership. He also teaches with Darul Uloom Fremont Seminary.

His work emphasizes balancing classical scholarship with contemporary community realities, helping Muslims strengthen faith, character, and service.
