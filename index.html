About

Imam Shadman Ahmed serves as Imam and Resident Scholar at Masjid Zakariya (ICF) in Fremont, California, with a focus on accessible Islamic learning, family-centered guidance, and principled community leadership.

His work emphasizes balancing classical scholarship with contemporary community realities, helping Muslims strengthen faith, character, and service.

Scholarship & Service

Khutbah & Public Lectures

Regular Friday sermons and thematic talks focused on worship, family life, ethics, and social responsibility.

Pastoral Care

Religious consultation, spiritual counseling, and support for individuals and families navigating personal and community challenges.

Community Development

Collaboration with local leaders and institutions to strengthen Muslim civic presence, youth development, and service culture.
